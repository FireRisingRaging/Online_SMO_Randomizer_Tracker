SMO Connection Map

  • Left Mouse Button hold & drag to connect stages.
  • Double Left Mouse Button to disconnect a path — this disconnects the stage connected to the one clicked. e.g. with Cascade→Sand→Lake→Wooded→Lost, double-clicking Lake gives you two paths: Wooded→Lost and Cascade→Sand→Lake.
  • Middle Mouse Button to mark/unmark a stage green. Right Mouse Button to mark/unmark a stage red.
  • Scroll to zoom, drag empty space to pan.
