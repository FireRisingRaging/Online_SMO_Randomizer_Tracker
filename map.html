SMO Connection Map

Controls
  • Left Mouse Button hold and drag a zone/kingdom to draw a connection arrow. A node can have any number of outgoing and incoming arrows.
  • Double Left Mouse Button on a node to remove ALL of its outgoing arrows at once.
  • Click an arrow on the map (or a path in the Display panel) to highlight that path on both; click again to unhighlight.
  • Middle Mouse Button to mark/unmark a stage green. Right Mouse Button to mark/unmark a stage red.
  • Drag the panel background or kingdom header to move a kingdom panel.
  • Triangle handle (bottom-right of each panel) to resize it. Zones reflow automatically in that space.
  • Single-click a node (with Stage Previews on) to view its loading zone screenshot.
  • Hover over an arrow to see what letter chain it is part of.
  • Single-click an arrow to view its chain and what chain it can be linked to or add it to a new chain.
  • Scroll to zoom, drag empty space to pan.
Controls
  • Hold and drag any zone or kingdom to draw a connection arrow. A node can have any number of outgoing and incoming arrows.
  • Double-tap a node to remove ALL of its outgoing arrows at once.
  • Tap a path in the Display panel to highlight it on the map; tap again to unhighlight.
  • Single tap a node to cycle its color: green → red → none.
  • Tap once a panel's empty background to select it (border lights up), then drag from that background to move it. Deactivates automatically when you lift your finger.
  • Triangle handle (bottom-right of each panel): drag to resize it. Zones reflow automatically.
  • Hold for 1 second on a node without moving (Stage Previews must be on) to view its loading zone screenshot. Moving your finger cancels it and starts a connection drag instead.
  • Pinch to zoom, drag empty canvas to pan.
Info
  • The striped loading zones represent one-ways, one entrance in or out.
