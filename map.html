SMO Connection Map

Controls
  • Left Mouse Button hold and drag a zone/kingdom to draw a connection arrow.
  • Double Left Mouse Button to disconnect a path. This disconnects the stage connected to the one clicked. e.g. with Cascade→Sand→Lake→Wooded→Lost, double-clicking Lake gives you two paths: Wooded→Lost and Cascade→Sand→Lake.
  • Middle Mouse Button to mark/unmark a stage green. Right Mouse Button to mark/unmark a stage red.
  • Drag the panel background or kingdom header to move a kingdom panel.
  • Triangle handle (bottom-right of each panel) to resize it. Zones reflow automatically in that space.
  • Single-click a node (with Stage Previews on) to view its loading zone screenshot.
  • Scroll to zoom, drag empty space to pan.
Info
  • The stripped loading zones represent one-ways, one entrance in or out
